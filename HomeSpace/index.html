Have Questions About Home Loans?

Weâ€™ve compiled answers to the most common questions about mortgages, eligibility, and the application process.

What is the minimum credit score required for a home loan?

Most lenders require a minimum credit score of 650, but better scores can secure lower interest rates. We also offer options for individuals with low credit scores.

How much down payment do I need?

Typically, a down payment of 10-20% of the property value is required. However, some loans allow as low as 5% depending on eligibility.

Can I get a home loan if I am self-employed?

Yes, we provide tailored home loan solutions for self-employed individuals based on income proof, business stability, and credit history.

What documents are required for the application?

Typically, you need ID proof, address proof, income documents (salary slips or ITR), and property documents. Our team will guide you through the process.

How long does it take to get loan approval?

With our fast-track process, you can get approval within 48 hours once all documents are submitted and verified.

Are there any hidden charges?

No hidden charges. All fees, including processing and service charges, will be transparently communicated before you proceed.
